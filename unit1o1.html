1.1 Describing the purpose of IT in modern business

IT has become a necessity for most modern businesses, whether it be a small shop using computers to order in new stock or take online orders, up to big corporations using IT for fast communication between teams and for planning out and creating their next big project. Even if the business itself is not heavily reliant on computers, IT can still help speed up a lot of processes, even if it is only used for things like market research and research in general, communication, and maybe even looking up help for a problem you are unable to fix yourself.


1.2 Describing methods, skills and resources needed to complete digital projects successfully – what do you need to make projects successful? What tools? What skills?

There are a lot skills, methods and resources needed to complete a digital project, at the start of a project one of the most important things is a good research method, making sure you have the information you need to complete the project, if this is not done then you could end up making a lot of mistakes that could have been avoided. A good skill for a project is time and project management, making sure you have enough time to complete the project and being able to make sure you and your team are completing the most important parts first.

One of the important parts of planning a project is to break it down into individual task in order to make it easier to see what exactly you have to do, this is also useful when it comes to time management as it will make it easier to see exactly how much work you need to do, this is known as the kanban method, Trello is very effect at doing this. 1.4 Describe the risks that might impact digital projects – what causes digital projects to fail? Why?

There are many risks when it comes to digital projects, a big risk is security, there can be situations where people could get access to your project by hacking into your online accounts and stealing your information, you could also get viruses that infect your computers and corrupt your files causing you to lose your project completely. Another big risk is the project not working due to the methods used to create it not working, like certain bits of code just not being able to work together or maybe another project has been completed by somebody else that just works better or offers more. These risks can be negated by increasing security and being careful with your accounts and doing more market research to make sure your project hasn’t already been done before.


1.5 Describe how you would go about selecting and using IT systems and software – how do you select the right kind of technology for the job?

When it comes to IT in modern business one of the key aspects is knowing what software to use for your project, this is very important since picking the wrong one or not using software at all could slow down or even stop your project in its tracks. A good method to figuring out what you’ll need is the kanban method, breaking down the project into tasks you can then see what you’ll need to complete each one. Another important part is to research what software would be best for the project, as you might already have one in mind but another piece of software might be more efficient at the job, saving yourself time.

1.7 Describe legal guidelines and constraints that impact digital projects – How does GDPR impact digital projects?

An important aspect of any Business or project is the legal guidelines that need to be followed, one of the most important guidelines is copyright infringement. When working on a project you need to make sure that whatever you’re making isn’t infringing on someone's copyrighted material, you can avoid this by making everything yourself and documenting it but if you need to use someone else work then you need to make sure that it is not copyrighted or that the copyright owner has given you written permission to use their work in your project.
